Accounts
Create Accounts
To use platform you must have an a account. You can have only one Personal Account and unlimited Business Accounts. If you want to join some existing Business Account select option Join to Business Account. All enabled Accounts will be listed in top right corner and you can switch easy from one to other.
Activate Personal Account
Personal account will be your default account and can be used to buy goods and services for personal needs. You can have only one Personal Account.
Please press Activate button and you will be redirected to small activation form.

Join to Business Account
If there already has Business Account of company you work you can easy sent Join Request. Persons with Join Request Accept Rights will receive your join request and will be able to accept it.
Please press Join button and provide correct personal information.

Create Business Account
If you are business owner or employee of organization which has no Business Account this option let you create new Business Account. Remember that merging 2 existing Business Acounts of the same organization can be tricky, so first, check does this organization already exists. Please provide correct organization data so your partners can recognize you.

Account Validation

Every account has validation level.

There are 4 validation levels. When new account is created default validation level is assigned to it. You have option to make validation at any level you want.

Validation levels is required to make safe deals with other users. As higher validation user has, as safer deals can be made.

It is up to you to make deals with low validation level account owner and we do not make any responsibility about descision you make.

Account Merging

Account merging currently is not available, but we have some ideas and we think to implement this in some future.
